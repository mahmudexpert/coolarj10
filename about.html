About

Fremont 3D Printing was started by Arjun Mehta as a way for local creators, makers, small businesses, and entrepreneurs to have easy access to testing their designs and developing their ideas. Mainstream manufacturers have long lead times, and their 3D printing quote generation tools often blow costs out of proportion for simpler projects. With operations based in Fremont, California, we hope to make 3D printing a viable option for everyone in the Bay Area!
